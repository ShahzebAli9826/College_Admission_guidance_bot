College Admission Guidance Chatbot:

Introduction:
Choosing the right career path after class 12 is one of the most critical and confusing decisions for students.
Many students lack proper guidance regarding available streams, suitable colleges, entrance examinations, and 
future career opportunities. Traditional counselling methods are limited, time-consuming, and not easily 
accessible to everyone.

To solve this problem, we propose an AI-Powered College Admission Guidance Chatbot that provides instant, 
accurate, and personalized guidance to students regarding academic streams, college options, entrance exams, 
and future career scope using Artificial Intelligence.

Problem Statement:
Students after class 12 face challenges such as:
1) Lack of awareness about different streams (Engineering, Medical, Commerce, Arts, etc.)
2)Confusion regarding entrance exams and eligibility
3)Limited access to professional counsellors
4)Difficulty in understanding future career scope

Hence, there is a need for a smart, accessible, and interactive guidance system.

Objective of the Project:
The main objectives of this project are:
1)To guide students about streams after 12th
2)To suggest college options and entrance exams
3)To explain career scope and future opportunities
4)To provide instant responses using LLM
5)To create a user-friendly web-based chatbot

Scope of the Project:
This chatbot can be used by:

1)Class 10th–12th students
2)Parents seeking guidance
3)Educational institutes for counselling support

Future scope includes:
1)Integration with real college databases
2)Personalized recommendations using student marks
3)Multilingual support
4)Image input
5)voice incput
